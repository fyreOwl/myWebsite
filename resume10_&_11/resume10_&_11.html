WHAT IS KINESIOLOGY?

A lot of the times we all get to roadblocks and we find ourselves in a One Way Street where we can't see a way out. Feeling stress and anxiety can cause serious damage to your health. This is your body's way of trying to send you messages that there is something wrong. Unfortunately not all of us are so attentative and we need a little nudge.

Kinesiology is the science of muscle testing. It is a gentle non-evasive way to listen to your body's need to be healthy and unlock it's ability to heal itself on an emotional, physical & chemical level.

Other disciplines only focuses in on one level, often physical, and do not take the emotional and chemical influences of the client's situation or issues in consideration.

The Kinesiologist do not diagnose

Instead, the Kinesiologist is a facilitator. Through muscle testing (Neuro Transmitting messages between the brain and muscle), the Kinesiologist helps the client to become aware of imbalances in body and to help the client with the healing process... on all levels.

This can relate to stress, sport injuries, nutrition and more.

THE BENEFITS

Relationships

Maintaining boundaries, trust, assertiveness, emotional intelligence, convictions, co-dependencies and prejudices.

If any of the above concepts is causing stress, Kinesiology provides you with powerful tools. You can become your optimum self within and independently from the relationship.

Depression

Depression is a clinical diagnoses, but there are times you feel you lost control. You are angry and irritable. Taking control can be exhausting and it feels like it's easier to just give up.

Kinesiology can help you find your energy and take control again.

Digestion

Digestion is one part of the six stages of nutrition, but if any of the six stages are impaired, toxins and heavy metal build up can occur, causing indigestion and other health problems.

Kinesiology can help you with a detoxification progam, lymph drainage and other required actions to assure that you maintain optimum health.

Pain - Muscle & Joint

From sport injuries, hypertonic muscles to posture problems, Kinesiology is ideal to help you.

Food Sensitivities

Sensitivity is the term Kinesiologist use instead of allergy. The term allergy is a medical diagnoses and Kinesiologists do not diagnose.

Kinesiology has many tools to help you overcome your food sensitivities and other health problems that occurred because of them.

Fatigue

Fatigue has many causes, but in itself can cause additional problems that can be detremental to your health. Fatigue needs to be addressed sooner than later.

Kinesiogy will help you to not only restore your vitality, but will put you on the path to optimum health.

Self-Esteem

A low self-esteem is extremely disabilitating. A viscious circle of negative self talk that leads to poor performance.

Kinesiology can help you break that circle so that can feel deserving, courageous and ambitious again. You will be able to trust yourself. And you will be able to achieve goals.

Phobias

Our fears are very real to us. Whether it is a fear of heights or a fear of being alone.

In Kinesiology, we help you explore these fears and provide you with tools to overcome them.

Addictions

Physical and emotional addictions can feel like chains hanging around your neck.

Kinesiology provides you with the tools to break these chains.
